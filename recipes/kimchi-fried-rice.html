Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice, or kimchi bokkeumbap, is an extremely popular dish from South Korea, made primarily with kimchi and rice, that is very quick and easy to make, taking only about 20 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 cup spicy kimchi, chopped
  • 3 cups cold leftover white rice
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped, divided
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds, divided
  • 4 large eggs, cooked over easy, soft-boiled, or fried

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add bacon and cook for 5 minutes. Add kimchi and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add rice and sesame oil. Cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer to serving dishes. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds. Top each dish with prepared egg of choice.
